ABOUT

CU Women in Computing (CUWIC) is the ACM-W chapter within the Computer Science Department at the University of Colorado at Boulder that is dedicated to supporting women and diversity in computing. We encourage interaction from all who are interested in issues regarding women and diversity in computing to create a forum in which to share information about career, academic, and social opportunities. We are mainly funded by the department of Computer Science.
